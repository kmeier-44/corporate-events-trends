SPENDING BENCHMARKS

What Companies Are Actually Spending

Median confirmed spend has more than doubled — from £2,818 in 2022 to £7,230 in 2025. The top quartile now exceeds £21,000. The market is maturing, with larger, higher-value events becoming the norm.

Price per head has risen in step, climbing from £63 to £102 over the same period. Budgets are growing, but so is the cost of delivering a great experience.

CATEGORY TRENDS

How the Event Mix Is Shifting

Conferences surged post-COVID but are normalising. Corporate parties and networking events are on the rise. Meetings are steadily declining as a share of the mix — companies are investing more in higher-impact event formats.
