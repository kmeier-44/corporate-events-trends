Hybrid Events

The Hybrid Trajectory

Once a buzzword, hybrid tagging peaked at 4.8% in 2022 and has since all but disappeared from enquiry specifications.

Explicit "hybrid" requests peaked at 4.8% in 2022 before dropping to near-zero by 2024. But this doesn't mean hybrid died.

The reality: hybrid capabilities — AV streaming, camera setups, remote participation tools — became standard venue infrastructure. Organisers stopped specifying it because they expect it.

For venues, the takeaway is clear: hybrid tech is now table stakes, not a differentiator. It's baked into how modern events operate.
